### Example Settings

Use a short orienting sentence when readers need context before the first subsection.

#### General

- **Display Name**

To rename an item, enter a new value in **Display Name**, then select **Save**.

If successful, the confirmation message **Changes saved** appears below the form.

- **Visibility**

To share the item with collaborators, enable **Visible to team**, then select **Save**.

!!! note "Use screenshots selectively"

    Add screenshots immediately after a step only when they reduce ambiguity, confirm a state, or highlight a control that is hard to find.

#### Advanced

- **API Endpoint**

Enter the endpoint URL in `https://example.org/api/...` format, then select **Validate**.

If validation succeeds, the status changes to **Connected**.
